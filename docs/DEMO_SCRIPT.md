# 90-second demo script

1. Open the dashboard and point out the ward-level heat-risk map.
2. Click an extreme-risk ward such as Sojati Gate.
3. Explain that the panel shows surface temperature, green cover, built-up density and the top risk drivers.
4. Set cool roofs to 45%, tree cover gain to 10% and shaded assets to 12.
5. Press **Run simulation** and show the before/after Heat Risk Score and action brief.
6. Download the import template to show that the platform can move from demo data to ward-level city data.
7. Close by saying: “HeatShield AI does not only detect heat; it prioritises the next cooling action.”
